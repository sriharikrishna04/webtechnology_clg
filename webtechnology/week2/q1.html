Food festivals typically feature a variety of food, including local dishes, street food, and produce: Local dishes: Food festivals often showcase local dishes and talent. Street food: Food festivals may include a range of street food from around the world. Produce: Food festivals can celebrate harvests and give thanks for a plentiful growing season. Food festivals can also include interactive installations, such as: DIY food craft stations and Oversized food-themed props for photo opportunities. Food festivals can benefit the community and the food industry in a number of ways, including: Attracting new customers to restaurants and food vendors, Promoting local cuisine, and Fostering a sense of community.
