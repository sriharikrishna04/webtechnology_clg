Photography is a hobby that involves capturing images with a camera, either digitally or on film. It's a creative way to express yourself by focusing on details, light, composition, and perspective. Many people enjoy photography as a way to document moments, explore nature, or simply capture the beauty of everyday life. Whether you're taking portraits, landscapes, or abstract photos, photography can be a fulfilling way to see the world through a different lens. It's also a great way to relax, practice mindfulness, and improve your technical skills over time.
